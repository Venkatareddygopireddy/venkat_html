GLOSSARY USING DEFINITION LIST

Taj- mahal
A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Agra, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Known for its white marble architecture
Jaipur- rajasthan
Capital of Rajasthan, also called the Pink City. Famous for its forts, palaces, and vibrant bazaars.
Varanasi
One of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities, located on the banks of the Ganges River. A major spiritual and pilgrimage site in India.
Kerala backwaters
A network of lagoons, lakes, and canals in the state of Kerala, popular for houseboat cruises and scenic beauty.
Ladakh- jammu & kashmir
A high-altitude region in northern India known for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist culture, and adventure tourism
